How to save parents' valuable time and energy & eliminate stress due to a complicated commute? Two concepts that revamp the daily routine for parents by addressing the central issue head-on.

Delivery

Comfy Self Driving Bubble Car to Daycare

We've developed autonomous friendly “Bubble Cars” to streamline the morning drop-offs, shuttling children safely to daycare centers across Lausanne.
These vehicles are tailored for young passengers, ensuring a secure and fun commute. Where necessary, a supervisor is onboard to monitor and interact with the children, enhancing the journey with care and entertainment.

Relocation

A mobile childcare van streamlining the journey for parents

The van efficiently picks up children from various places convenient for the parent. Once all the kids are on board, it relocates to a designated childcare hub to function as an outdoors daycare.
The van is designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and engaging environment during transit. Ensuring they arrive at the childcare hub content and ready for the day.

The concept of virtual site probing

As the architecture industry takes on more renovation and renewal work rather than new builds in most parts of the world, building documentation for the design workflow takes up more and more of architect's time.
Especially with consideration of poorly architecturally documented heritage buildings and unconsolidated databases of past renovations/rework, architects spend a large portion of their time reconstructing the space to stay updated with current design technology while doing a lot of detective work for poorly documented spaces.
This often requires site visits and travel time especially for international architects.

Using remote controlled robots as architectural site probing devices

Our solution introduces a virtual site visitation tool for architects, empowering them with the freedom to explore and document on-site details remotely.
Utilizing precise aerial maneuverability and powerful scanning, this drone accurately reconstructs site conditions, along with information on specific materials and architectural styles.

Styling a  Medical  Patent

A Fashionable & Discreet Neck Brace Clothing

Bridging the gap between medical necessity and personal style, our fashionable and discreet neck brace clothing redefines the experience of wearing a medical device.

Objectives

We aim to provide a medically effective neck support that stabilizes the cervical spine after strain or injury.
The inclusion to stylish clothing ensures the neck brace is discreet, allowing users to conceal their medical condition if they choose so.

Why?

One may be conscious about their appearance and social perceptions.
Some cervical spine related injuries and problems may require a long time to heal.
Additionally, some people with lifestyle-induced neck conditions may prefer not to broadcast health issues to the public.

Empowering choice in medical fashion, we tailor neck brace designs to individual needs & tastes

We offer various designs, which allow individuals to choose the extent to which they wish to disguise their neck brace.
This selection process also serves as a chance to consider the suitability of a person's environment for a more concealed design, or alternatively, the necessity of a more noticeable design that alerts others to exercise caution in their vicinity, ensuring a safer environment for everyone involved.
Based on Medical Patent CN 208863020 U
"Wearable Neck Device"

Our design acknowledges social stigma around visible medical apparatus and lifestyle-induced conditions.

This approach — which combines style and medical devices — respects the user's need for personal expression and comfort during recovery, offering a range of options that look like everyday clothing for a more confident healing journey.

“Context”

In urban environments, the focus leans heavily towards ensuring the satisfaction of people, sometimes overlooking the specific requirements of our canine companions. Dogs, just like people, need safe, comfortable, and accessible infrastructures.
Challenged to integrate cities and contextualization, we selected Moscow, known for its harsh weather and numerous stray dogs as our canvas. Our projects aim to enhance the lives of these dogs through thoughtful architectural interventions:

1. To Stay Warm

We designed shelters atop market ventilation shafts, utilizing the warm air they emit. This provides a cosy, private space for stray dogs like Lulu, a Russian Spaniel with puppies, ensuring they stay warm during Moscow's cold winters.

2. To Cross

Along the banks of the Moskva River, we create a dog-specific passage beneath the city's bridges, providing a quiet and safe route for stray dogs to navigate the urban environment. This path shields them from the noise and hazards of traffic, offering a secure and calm transit space.

3. To Find Home

Community involvement is key. We want to encourage citizens to take a more empathetic view towards stray dogs. Taking advantage of the unusual nature of the subways that are filled with dogs, passengers become the caretakers, with the subway providing food stations, water sources, and safe resting areas.
This creates a unique bond between commuters and the stray dogs, turning it into compassion and collective responsibility.

Collage in the context of Urban Spaces

Gurzelen Stadium: Canvas for Urban Renewal

Once a lively football field, Bienne's Gurzelen Stadium, abandoned in 2016, now stands as an open space brimming with potential. Its vast, flat expanse offers a unique opportunity for creative urban redevelopment.
Our project aims to transform this large, dormant area into a dynamic community hub, redefining its role in the urban landscape. Gurzelen's transformation is set to demonstrate how abandoned spaces can be reimagined into vibrant centers of activity and innovation.

Mixing & combining concepts

Drawing inspiration from the vibrant atmosphere of lively bars, food festivals, and chill-out zones, the transformation of Gurzelen Stadium aims to infuse these elements into a collage that reshapes it into an effervescent and festive urban retreat.
